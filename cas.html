Overview

Full rebranding work including logos, leaflets, applications, merchandises, etc. on the California Academy of Sciences Museum.

This place is an aquarium, planetarium, rainforest, and natural history museum in the heart of San Francisco’s Golden Gate Park—and a powerful voice for biodiversity research and exploration, environmental education, and sustainability across the globe.

Inspiration

Logo

The museum is largely divided into rain forest, natural history museum, aquarium, and planetarium. I made a symbol for each part and set the whole logo concept.
