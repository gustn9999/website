UX Design

STOCK X

REDESIGN PROJECT

Type Inclass project
Year 2020 spring
Category
  • UX Design

Overview

Main feature

It helps facilitate transactions between people who own limited-edition clothes. The prices of shoes naturally fluctuate with changes in demand and supply.

Design Goal

Fast

Reduce the time it takes to find the desired product.

Social

Refer to celebrities and others lookbook on social media.

Clean

The visual design of layout should be refined.

Audience Research

Research has found that many users are influenced by SNS and influencers to purchase items. Also, the demand for Asian and European users was different, and in common, They bought streetwear to look comfortable and cool.
