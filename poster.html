Overview

Movie poster artworks using photography and Adobe Photoshop.

I set a virtual film to produce movie posters. The theme of the movie is romance and action, and it features myself and friends as characters. All the pictures were taken by myself.

Photograhy

Muni

Romance movie poster

I imagined a romance in the background of transportation, Muni. This could be just a means of transportation that people in San Francisco routinely use, but sometimes, everyone imagines a special romance that happens in it.

Rival

Action movie poster

I designed posters imagining action movies like hitmen. I wore a suit that I usually wear, and I had my friends wear my uniform and accessories.
