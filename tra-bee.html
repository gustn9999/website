UX Design

TRA-BEE

Type Inclass project
Year 2020 spring
Category
  • UX Design

Overview

Traffic Regulation Drone System

Tra-bee is a drone system to assist traffic police officers in outdoor work. This system consists of a battery charge deck, drone, and application that can control.

Problems

Deaths

The outdoor traffic regulation work causes many deaths of police officers.

High cost

Large numbers of CCTV and police officers are needed to crack down on large areas.

Slow

When traffic problems occur, it takes a lot of time for police personnel to be deployed. As a result, it takes a lot of time to solve the traffic problem.

Play

Research

I could see many traffic police officers who are exposed to risk. In addition, human activities on traffic regulation miss many things and are more likely to be affected by the surrounding environment such as weather, physical condition, and fatigue than machines.

A study conducted by the National Law Enforcement Officers Memorial Fund examined the deaths of 684 police officers over a five-year period found that 272 officers died from being in an automobile crash or being struck by an automobile. Now that technology is evolving, we need systems that can reduce their risk and sacrifice.

Affinity Diagram

To get an in-depth analysis of the qualitative research data, I used Affinity diagramming. This phase helped me to categorize and prioritize the data of problems the police officers faced.

Persona

I created Personas to represent a user group that faces similar problems. This phase gave me a clear picture of the user's expectations and how they would like to use a product.
